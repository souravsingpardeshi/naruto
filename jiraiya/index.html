Pervy Sage

Jiraiya (自来也)

was one of Konohagakure's Sannin. Famed as a hermit and
pervert of stupendous ninja skill, Jiraiya travelled the world
in search of knowledge that would help his friends, the
various novels he wrote, and, posthumously, the world in
its entirety – knowledge that would be passed on to his
godson and final student, Naruto Uzumaki.
